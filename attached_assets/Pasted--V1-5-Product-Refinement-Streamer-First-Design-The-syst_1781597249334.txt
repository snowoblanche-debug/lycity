# V1.5 Product Refinement - Streamer First Design

The system is already functional, but I want to optimize it for real-world streamer usage.

My priorities:

1. Streamer convenience
2. Google Sheet as the single source of truth
3. Long-term maintainability
4. Better readability and UX
5. Future scalability

---

## GOOGLE SHEET SHOULD BE THE MASTER DATABASE

I do NOT want to maintain songs in two places.

Google Sheet should be the source of truth.

Admin panel should sync from Google Sheet.

Workflow:

Google Sheet
↓
Sync Songs
↓
Update Supabase
↓
Website updates automatically

---

## GOOGLE SHEET STRUCTURE

Column A = 歌名

Column B = 歌手

Column C = 語種

Column D = 標籤

Column E = 狀態

Examples:

Ring Ring Ring | 123 | 中文 | #抒情,#RAP | 已解鎖

Jour un | | 小語種 | #EMO,#外文 | 🐣修練中

起風了 | | 中文 | #抒情,#破音警告 | 已解鎖

Mapping:

title = A
artist = B
language = C
tags = D
status = E
